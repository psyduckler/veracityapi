# Video M1 quality gate — 2026-05-13

## Scope

Private-beta `/v1/analyze-video` MVP using:

- Direct downloadable HTTPS video URLs only.
- External zero-idle ffmpeg extractor returning one 3x2 JPEG contact sheet plus sanitized metadata.
- Claude Haiku 4.5 vision scoring via Anthropic.
- `$0.05` successful-analysis billing bucket `video_v0`.
- No raw video, frames, contact sheets, base64 payloads, or full URLs stored by default.
